Grant Prospector ADE — Live Prototype

A working demonstration of an Autonomous Digital Employee deployed for Hope & Harmony Farms' grant and subsidy operation. Aquila — codename for the deployed ADE — continuously monitors 47 federal, state, and private funding sources, pre-screens eligibility against the operation, drafts applications, and manages post-award reporting and drawdowns. Submission authority remains with the owner. Built across five progressive phases — Phase 5 is the recommended starting point.

5
Phase 5: Settings & Integrations Recommended for Demo
Production-readiness layer: 11 connected integrations (Grants.gov OAuth, SAM.gov, USDA eAuth, VDACS, NRCS, QuickBooks, John Deere Operations Center, Microsoft 365, DocuSign), team & RBAC, notification preferences, SOC 2 Type II security posture, and itemized billing. All earlier features included.

4
Phase 4: Award Lifecycle Manager
Post-award administration as a fourth top-level view. Awarded programs table with drawdown progress, cumulative drawdown chart (planned vs. actual), Tobacco Region Grant performance metrics tracking, and 12-month reporting calendar with Aquila-prepared reports.

3
Phase 3: Financial Impact & Compliance Center
Top-nav view switcher introduced. Financial Impact view: 12-month capital flow chart, before/after ADE comparison, per-program ROI table, cost-of-capital comparison. Compliance Center: registrations, certifications, match obligations, and full audit trail with category filter.

2
Phase 2: Application Editor & Approval Flow
Full-screen Application Editor workspace (section nav, draft editor, AI Assistant suggestions, comments, reviewer criteria). Filter & search controls on the opportunity stream. Owner approval dialog reinforcing the human-in-the-loop submission model. Document upload modal for pending owner-required items.

Phase 1: Operations Console Foundation
Core operating dashboard with master Autonomous Mode toggle, live opportunity stream auto-injecting from a pool of 22 real federal/state/private programs, four-tab drill-down per opportunity (Overview / Eligibility Match / Draft Application / Activity Log / Award History), source coverage panel, and operational calendar.
